Our Services

Managed software services for the automotive domain

Our managed services are specifically designed to address the unique needs of the automotive industry, focusing on secure and reliable software management that supports connected vehicles, telematics, and autonomous driving systems.

How we engage

Flexible engagement models tailored to the demands of each automotive project.

Offshore Development

Dedicated offshore automotive software teams that deliver to your specifications, standards and timelines while reducing development cost.

In House Development

Engineering delivered from our own facility, with established processes and infrastructure for automotive-grade software.

Managed Software Services

End-to-end managed development and validation of automotive software, owned and delivered as a complete service.

Staff Augmentation

Skilled automotive engineers embedded in your team for short- or long-term projects, without the overhead of permanent hiring.

Development and validation, end to end

Embedded Software Development

Embedded programming for ECUs, ADAS, infotainment, telematics, and vehicle diagnostics.

Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X)

V2X communication protocols that connect vehicles with infrastructure, other vehicles, and pedestrians.

Software Testing and Validation

Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) and software-in-the-loop (SIL) testing for functionality and reliability.

Cloud-Based Integration

Scalable cloud solutions for real-time data processing, connected car services and over-the-air updates.

Compliance and Safety Standards

Adherence to ISO 26262 and ASPICE, keeping software safe, secure and compliant.

Model Based Development & Simulation

Model-based design and simulation to streamline development and meet high precision requirements.
