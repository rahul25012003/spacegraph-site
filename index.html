Our software development and validation services

Our services are designed to address the unique needs of the automotive industry, focusing on secure and reliable software that supports connected vehicles, telematics, and autonomous driving systems, and helping OEMs and suppliers accelerate product development while reducing costs and risks.

Embedded Software Development

Proficient in embedded system programming for ECUs, ADAS, infotainment, telematics, and vehicle diagnostics in the automotive sector.

Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Solutions

Developing V2X communication protocols that enable connected vehicles to interact with infrastructure, other vehicles, and pedestrians.

Software Testing and Validation

Comprehensive testing and validation services, including hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) and software-in-the-loop (SIL) testing, to ensure functionality and reliability.

Cloud-Based Integration

Scalable cloud solutions for real-time data processing and integration, supporting connected car services and over-the-air updates.

Compliance and Safety Standards

Adherence to stringent automotive industry standards like ISO 26262 and ASPICE, ensuring our clients' software solutions are safe, secure, and compliant.

Model Based Development & Simulation

Expertise in model-based design and simulation to streamline automotive software development and meet high precision requirements.
